Adele children: Meet Angelo Adkins

Adele’s son Angelo Adkins is one of those famous kids who get little attention, and his mother prefers it that way.

Even though the 9-year-old has grown up with one of the most well-known music singers in the world as a mother, Adele has made it a priority to keep her kid out of the spotlight.
