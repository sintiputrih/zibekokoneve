Yūki Kaji (Japanese: 梶裕貴 Kaji Yūki) is a Japanese voice actor for the Pokémon anime.

Biography

Kaji was born in Tokyo, Japan on September 3, 1985 and became a voice actor and singer as an adult. Kaji began to provide voices for the Pokémon anime near the end of Pokémon the Series: Black & White, where he portrayed Pokémon rescuer Virgil. Shortly afterwards, he gained more prominent roles in Pokémon the Series: XY, playing Clemont, one of Ash's traveling partners, and the Zygarde nicknamed Squishy and Z2. Kaji is currently affiliated with the talent group VIMS.
