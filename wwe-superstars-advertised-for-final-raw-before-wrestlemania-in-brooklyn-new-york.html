WWE Superstars Advertised for Final RAW Before WrestleMania In Brooklyn, New York

WWE has officially announced that the last episode of Raw before WrestleMania 40 will be held at the Barclays Center in Brooklyn, New York, on April 1.

The event will feature a lineup of top WWE Superstars, including Seth Rollins, Cody Rhodes, CM Punk, Becky Lynch, Rhea Ripley, Finn Balor, Damian Priest, Dominik Mysterio, Jey Uso, and Drew McIntyre.

Tickets for this highly anticipated event will be available for purchase starting on Friday, January 19 at 10:00 am local time. Wrestling fans in the New York area can look forward to an exciting night of action as WWE builds momentum towards WrestleMania 40.
