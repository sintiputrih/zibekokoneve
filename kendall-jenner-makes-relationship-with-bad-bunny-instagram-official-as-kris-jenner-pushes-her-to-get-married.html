Kendall Jenner Makes Relationship With Bad Bunny Instagram Official As Kris Jenner Pushes Her To Get

Highlights

  • Kendall Jenner seemingly confirms her relationship with Bad Bunny in an Instagram post featuring him in the background at a pool party.
  • Kendall is known for being private about her romantic life, so this is a surprising move from her.
  • Despite speculation and her mother's hopes for her to settle down and have kids, Kendall has expressed that she is happy staying child-free for now and wants to keep her relationship with Bad Bunny private.

After months of speculation, it looks like Kendall Jenner is finally confirming her relationship with Bad Bunny on Instagram.

Kendall just made a post to Instagram with a series of recent photos, captioning it, “Peanut and beer.” In photo eight, eagle-eyed fans noticed Bad Bunny spotted in the background at a pool party. He appears to be manning the barbecue.

This is the first time Kendall has posted anything referencing the rapper to social media, which isn’t surprising, given that she’s notoriously private about her romantic life.

Kris Jenner Wants Kendall To Get Married And Have Kids Soon

Bad Bunny and Kendall have only been romantically linked since February, but there’s been a ton of speculation around the pair, and Kris Jenner’s vocalness about her hopes for Kendall settling down soon haven’t helped.

During the season three finale of The Kardashians, Kendall revealed her mom gave her a custom dollhouse with an interesting surprise inside – an engagement ring. “She put an engagement ring in mine because she thought it was manifesting an engagement for me,” Kendall revealed to the camera. “So if that tells you anything about where my mom wants me to be in life.”

While several of Kendall’s siblings have yet to walk down the aisle – including Rob Kardashian and Kylie Jenner – Kendall is the only one who doesn’t have kids yet.

Kris has also made it clear on numerous occasions she hopes Kendall has kids sooner than later. For example, in February 2022, Kris was asked during an appearance on The Ellen DeGeneres show which of her kids she thinks is going to give her another grandchild.

In response, Kris said it would be “nice” to see Kendall have the family’s next baby. “She’s the only one who hasn’t had a baby,” Kris said, adding she believes the model “would eventually love to have a baby.”

Kendall Jenner Says She Wants Kids, But Not Right Now

Even though Kendall and Bad Bunny seem more serious than ever, Kris may still be waiting a while to see Kendall have children of her own, as the model has said she’s happy staying child-free for the time being.

In 2018, Kendall said she planned to “wait a little while longer” to have her own kids. The next year, she admitted to going through “phases” of wanting to settle down. “I think I have the fever,” she said, but added it’s not going to happen for a while at this point.

More recently, in a 2023 interview with Entertainment Tonight, Kendall says she plans to leave LA when the time comes for her to start having children.

"Oh, yeah. You heard it here first," she said. "I'm excited for that time in my life. I just know it’s not right now."

Kendall also addressed her desire to keep her relationship with Bad Bunny private, adding, "I try [to] find the balance of keeping things private and keeping things sacred, [and] also not letting the unfortunate frustration and stress of everyone trying to get in on it stop me from enjoying my side. Does that make sense?"

"I'll go out of my way to do things as privately as possible because I just think that that's the healthier way of dealing with relationships anyway,” Kendall continued.

Needless to say, it may be a while before fans get anymore updates on Kendall’s romantic life.
