Patrick has spent over two decades studying and tracking oil markets and fuel costs. Patrick has a degree in Business Economics from DePaul University.

He had focused on downstream oil markets, such as crude oil, gasoline, diesel, and jet fuel, since 2005, when he and a professor from Grand Valley State University started a blog to share oil market insights and forecast gas price movements.

Patrick went to work at GasBuddy, a fuel-price tracking service that provides information to millions of customers with a solid base.
