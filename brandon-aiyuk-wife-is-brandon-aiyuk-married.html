Brandon Aiyuk Wife: Is Brandon Aiyuk Married?

Brandon Aiyuk is an American football wide receiver and return specialist for the San Francisco 49ers of the National Football League.

Brandon Aiyuk was born on March 17, 1998, in Rocklin, California, United States. He grew up in Reno, Nevada.

Brandon Aiyuk attended Robert McQueen High School where he played wide receiver, defensive back and returned kicks for the high school football team. He was named first-team All-Northern Nevada and honourable mention All-State as a senior.

Brandon Aiyuk began his collegiate football career at Sierra College. He later committed to transfer to Arizona State University for the final two years of his NCAA eligibility.

Brandon Aiyuk was selected by the San Francisco 49ers in the first round with the 25th pick in the 2020 NFL Draft after they acquired the pick from the Minnesota Vikings in a trade. On June 26, 2020, He signed a four-year $12.5 million contract with the team, with a $6.6 million signing bonus.

Is Brandon Aiyuk Married?
