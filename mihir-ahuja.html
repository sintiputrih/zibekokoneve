Wiki/Biography

Mihir Ahuja celebrates his birthday on 28 June every year. He was born in Jamshedpur, Jharkhand. His zodiac sign is Cancer. Mihir Ahuja completed his school education at Carmel Junior School, Jamshedpur. Later, Mihir Ahuja went to HR College of Commerce and Economics, Mumbai to pursue a graduate degree.

Career

Mihir Ahuja made his Hollywood debut in 2000 with the film Cheaters. It was a Hollywood film and was directed by Jeffrey Gee Chin. In 2017, he was one of the top five contestants of the Oppo Times Fresh Face organised in Mumbai. In 2019, he made his debut in the web series titled ‘Up Starts’ on Netflix. In the same year, he appeared in the Bollywood film Super 30. Indian actor Hrithik Roshan was the lead actor in this film, which was directed by Vikas Bahl. During the same time, he was cast in the television series Bard of Blood. In the following year, he worked in the film ‘Yeh Ballet.’ Yeh Ballet was directed by Sooni Taraporevala. In 2020, he worked in the film ‘Dolly Kitty Aur Woh Chamakte Sitaare’ opposite Vikrant Massey and Bhumi Pednekar. Alankrita Srivastava was the director of the film ‘Dolly Kitty Aur Woh Chamakte Sitaare.’ In 2021, he appeared in the film State of Siege: Temple Attack. Later, he worked in several web series including Mission Over The Mars, Taj Mahal 1989, Engineering Girls Season 2, and Feels Like Ishq.
