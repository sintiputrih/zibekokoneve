Kevin Costner Seen Spending Time With New Woman Weeks After Finalizing Divorce

Highlights

  • Actor Kevin Costner spotted with a new woman, rumored to be his personal assistant, just weeks after finalizing his divorce from Christine Baumgartner.
  • Costner's ex-wife lost big in the divorce settlement, receiving significantly less in monthly support than she had initially requested.
  • Costner's court filing suggests that Baumgartner had been receiving financial assistance from her family and a new boyfriend, which influenced the court's decision.

It’s only been a matter of weeks since Kevin Costner filed his divorce from Christine Baumgartner after months of arguing over the terms of their divorce as well as custody of their three children. But it appears the actor is already moving on, as he was spotted spending one-on-one time with a new woman.

Kevin May Be Dating His Personal Assistant

Photos taken of Kevin on Saturday, October 28, showed the Yellowstone star leaving shopping center with a huge Wunderkind bag. Kevin was dressed casually in a grey t-shirt plus white pants and sneakers. He his famous face behind a pair of black shades.

Shortly behind him, a woman was seen carrying two wrapped gift boxes, as the duo went towards their waiting vehicle. Though she hasn’t been identified, Hollywood Life reported that she’s Kevin’s personal assistant. The woman, whose name hasn’t been publicly confirmed, was dressed in an all-black look with brown loafers. She could be seen loading the purchases into the trunk of Kevin’s car before they took off.

Kevin and Christine argued for months over finances after she filed for divorce in May. The couple only finalized the terms a month ago after months of battling in court.

“It is with great sadness that circumstances beyond his control have transpired which have resulted in Mr. Costner having to participate in a dissolution of marriage action. We ask that his, Christine’s, and their children’s privacy be respected as they navigate this difficult time,” Kevin’s representative said in a statement following Christine’s divorce filing in May.

Though Kevin and Christine’s divorce has been finalized, the terms of their agreement are being kept private. The pair did sign a prenup prior to their 2003 wedding, which entitled Christine to a million-dollar payout. However, it stipulated Kevin was to retain full ownership of their family home, which he bought a decade prior to their wedding.

Christine was originally refusing to move out of the home, which Kevin said defied their prenup, while she claimed she didn’t have the finances to relocate. Christine has since left the home for a rental property.

Kevin’s Ex-Wife Lost Big During Their Divorce

The parents-of-three also agreed to a child support and custody agreement in mid-September. Christine was awarded much less than she was initially seeking in monthly support from her ex-husband. The court decided that Kevin will provide Christine with $63,209 monthly, as opposed to her initial request for $161,592.

The exes are parents to three teenagers: Cayden, 16, Hayes, 14, and Grace, 13.

During the child support proceedings, Kevin pointed out that Christine has been getting financial assistance from her family as well as a new boyfriend, which seems to have factored into the court’s decision to award her much less than she expected.

“Christine’s attack [on me] is a smoke screen to deflect attention from her lack of candor regarding her own financial situation,” Kevin’s court filing argued. “Christine was forced to admit at her deposition that her brother recently gave her $80,000 and her boyfriend recently gave her $20,000.”

For now, Christine hasn’t publicly commented on the speculation that Kevin is dating someone new following their separation.
