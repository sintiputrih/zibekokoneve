American Model, Paulina Gretzky Got Engaged To Partner, Dustin Johnson In 2013

Canadian-American socialite and model, Paulina Gretzky is best known for being the daughter of the greatest hockey player of all time, Wayne Gretzky. Lately, she is gaining limelight as the longtime fiancee of an American professional golfer, Dustin Johnson.

As a singer, Paulian has released her own entitled single Collecting Dust, which emerged on Laguna Beach: The Real Orange County. Today, let's find out more interesting details including her love life in the below sections. 

Paulina Gretzky Got Engaged in 2013

Aged 32, Paulina Gretzky has been happily engaged to partner Dustin Johnson, 36, since 2013. They first met in 2009, when Dustin came over a family dinner after playing pro-am with Paulina's mother, Janet.

But right after the first encounter, Dustin and Paulina did not start their romantic relationship. As per the report, the pair officially began dating in late 2012.

Afterwards, in August 2013, Dustin got down one knee and happily proposed to Gretzky with an engagement ring. The golfer Dustin shared a picture of Paulina's hand wearing a diamond ring captioning, "She said yes", on his Instagram account on August 18, 2013.

However, the engaged duo claims the right time for marriage has yet to come up due to their busy schedules. Paulina said: " It'll be our special day, and we just want it to be right and to fit". 

Two Children- Tatum and River Johnson

Yes, Paulina and Dustin are yet to share their wedding vow. But they are already proud parents of their two beautiful baby boys. They first welcomed their firstborn kid, an eldest child Tatum Gretzky Johnson on January 19, 2015.

Similarly, the couple shared their second son named River Jones Johnson on June 12, 2017. Today, the Johnson brothers are living contentedly with their beloved and celebrity parents. 

Further, they visit many exotic places with their parents and can also be spotted in the post updated on their social sites.

How Much Is Paulina Gretzky in 2020?

The 1.73 m tall Paulina Gretzky has an impressive net worth of $5 million as of 2020. She has earned this stunning amount throughout her modelling and singing career. Along with this, she is quite active on social account and has 977k followers on her Instagram account.

Meanwhile, her partner, Dustin Johnson, the 193 cm tall, has a net worth of around $50 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. He has accumulated his fortune from his professional career as a golfer. He is playing as a professional golfer since 2007.

Johnson has competed in major championships such as Masters Tournament, PGA Championship, U.S. Open, World Golf Championship, RBC Canadian Open, and The Open Championship.

Real Estate

The Johnson's family currently reside at their mansion in Palm Beach Gardens, Florida. Johnson purchases the house back in 2015 for $4.95 million. 

As per the report, in 2017, Dustin also acquired a property in the Harbour Isle area of North Palm Beach. 

Bio and Early Life

Paulina Gretzky was born on December 19, 1988, in Los Angeles, California, United States. She is the firstborn kid of five children to Wayne Gretzky and Janet Jones. Her dad, Wayne is a famous ice hockey player while her mother, Janet is a movie actress.

Moreover, Gretzky has four younger siblings, a sister Emma Gretzky, and three brothers, Trevor Gretzky, Tristan Gretzky and Ty Gretzky. Concerning her education, she studied at Nightingale-Bamford School, located in New York City, New York.
