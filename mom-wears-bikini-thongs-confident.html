I love my mom bod - I 'celebrate' it by wearing micro bikinis and thong bottoms

ONE mother has taken to social media to celebrate her mom-bod.

She's doing so by sharing that micro bikinis and thong bottoms are trademarks of her wardrobe.

All smiles, in a TikTok recording, mama bear Sarah (@mommysonthenaughtylist) had a handful of varying underwear in her hands that ranged in style and color.

"Welcome to the side of TikTok where we celebrate our mom bodies by collecting: microbikinis, thong bikinis, slingshot one pieces and more," she wrote in her clip.

She had some support in her comments section from people that were on board with moms flaunting their figures in whatever they please.

"Here for it," one person encouraged.
