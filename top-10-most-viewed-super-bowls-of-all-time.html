Top 10 Most Viewed Super Bowls of All Time

The Super Bowl is not only a sporting event but also a cultural phenomenon, attracting millions of viewers each year. Here are the top 10 most viewed Super Bowls of all time, based on the ratings and viewership.

1. Super Bowl LVII (2023) - 115.1 million viewers

The most-watched U.S.-based telecast in history, the Kansas City Chiefs' 38-35 victory over the Philadelphia Eagles surpassed the previous record set in 2015, making it the most viewed Super Bowl of all time.

2. Super Bowl XLIX (2015) - 114.4 million viewers

This game between the New England Patriots and the Seattle Seahawks had held the record for the most viewers until it was surpassed by Super Bowl LVII.

3. Super Bowl LI (2017) - Nearly 113.7 million viewers

The matchup between the Patriots and the Falcons ranks as the third highest-rated telecast in Super Bowl history.

4. Super Bowl 50 (2016) - 111.9 million viewers

The game between the Denver Broncos and the Carolina Panthers comes in as the fourth most viewed Super Bowl of all time.

5. Super Bowl LII (2018) - 103.4 million viewers

The Philadelphia Eagles' victory over the New England Patriots marked one of the most-watched Super Bowls in history.

6. Super Bowl LIII (2019) - 98.2 million viewers

The New England Patriots faced the Los Angeles Rams in a game that attracted a sizable viewership, earning it a spot among the top 10 most viewed Super Bowls.

7. Super Bowl 50 (2016) - 111.9 million viewers

The clash between the Denver Broncos and the Carolina Panthers ranks as the seventh most watched Super Bowl of all time.

8. Super Bowl XLVIII (2014) - 112.2 million viewers

This game between the Seattle Seahawks and the Denver Broncos was one of the most-watched Super Bowls in history, securing its place among the top 10 most viewed Super Bowls.

9. Super Bowl XLVII (2013) - 108.7 million viewers

The Baltimore Ravens' victory over the San Francisco 49ers attracted a substantial viewership, making it one of the most viewed Super Bowls of all time.

10. Super Bowl XLVI (2012) - 111.3 million viewers

The game between the New York Giants and the New England Patriots rounds off the top 10 most viewed Super Bowls, drawing a massive audience.

The Super Bowl has consistently drawn massive viewership over the years, with each game offering a unique blend of sporting excitement, entertainment, and cultural significance. These top 10 most viewed Super Bowls have left an indelible mark on the history of television and sports entertainment, captivating audiences across the nation and beyond.
