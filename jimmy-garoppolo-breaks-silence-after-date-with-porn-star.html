Jimmy Garoppolo realizes he's 'under a microscope' after date with porn star

Jimmy Garoppolo isn’t dishing much about his recent date with porn star Kiara Mia, simply calling the outing a “learning experience.”

Speaking at training camp Wednesday, the 49ers quarterback was asked about his meet-up with the 41-year-old adult film star, and he said he’s still adjusting to life in the spotlight.

“Life is different now,” Garoppolo told reporters. “My life off the field, I’ve never really been big on being very public with things. Even social media, I’m not out there a ton. My life’s looked at differently. I’m under a microscope.”

Following the San Francisco starter’s dinner with Mia, alleged girlfriend (or former girlfriend) Alexandra King quickly scrubbed a Valentine’s Day selfie of Garoppolo, 26, on Instagram. He previously told Bleacher Report that while others reported they were dating, he wasn’t even aware the pair were an item.

“It’s like [coach] Kyle [Shanahan] said: It’s a good learning experience. I just have to take it in stride,” Garoppolo said, according to SF Gate.

As for Shanahan, he isn’t privy to speaking about his players’ personal lives.

“I’ve never in my life commented on a player’s date in July, so I’m not going to start today,” he said. “I don’t think it really pertains or matters to us.”

Wins, however, do matter, and Garoppolo managed to tally five in the W column last season, after being shipped to the Niners from the Patriots. He inked a five-year, $137.5 million deal in February.
