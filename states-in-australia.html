States In Australia And Capital Cities

The Northern Territory (NT), which is home to the capital city Darwin, sits at the northernmost tip of Australia.

This region elevates the outback experience to new heights, boasting some of the country’s top national parks, including

  • Kakadu National Park,
  • King’s Canyon, and
  • Litchfield National Park.

However, the most iconic destination is undoubtedly Uluru.

For anyone seeking a quintessential Australian experience featuring breathtaking landscapes,

emblematic wildlife, and authentic Aboriginal culture, the Northern Territory is an absolute must-visit.
