Yellowstone's Kelly Reilly's house in 'favourite city' 3,400miles from 'home'

The Bethany Dutton actress lives in New York

    Yellowstone's Kelly Reilly, 45, plays Bethany Dutton in the hit show and in real life she is married to financier Kyle Baugher and they live together in New York.

    Kelly originally grew up in Chessington, relocating to America for her career and now she's fully settled there.

    The actress once told Screen Rant: "I live in America, and I've been working for the past few years on a show called Yellowstone, where I play a very fierce character."

    But England will always be the star's home, as she made clear in one newspaper interview.

    Speaking to The Guardian in 2014, she said: "England is always home, but I have made my life here.

    "I suffered terrible homesickness at first — for English people, pubs, humour, all my family and friends — but I love New York, it's my favourite city."

    The actress likes to keep her private life on the down-low and there are no pictures inside her home.

    The star spends a lot of time away from her New York home for her filming commitments. From the fourth season, Yellowstone was filmed in Montana, and just one glance at Kelly's Instagram feed will show you her beautiful surroundings.

    Other Yellowstone stars have opted to live in Montana for ease, and that's where Luke Grimes resides with his Brazilian model wife Bianca Rodrigues Grimes.

    Paramount series fans have struggled to believe Kelly's nationality after hearing her real accent.

    Another fan said: "Mmm, excuse me, that's not what Beth sounds like."

    Many fans also didn't recognise her when a vintage snap resurfaced of Kelly with red cropped hair inside of her signature long blonde locks.
