Chad Johnson, Karina Smirnoff, More Join 'Famously Single'

Get the sweet potatoes ready! The Bachelorette season 12 villain Chad Johnson is among the famous faces heading to relationship rehab for E!’s second season of Famously Single, the network announced on Wednesday, November 2.

The unscripted series, focusing on eight celebs living together in Los Angeles in an effort to fix their dating woes, returns with new episodes in 2017. Joining “Bad Chad” are Karina Smirnoff (Dancing With the Stars), Malika Haqq (Dash Dolls), Ronnie Ortiz-Magro (Jersey Shore), David Mcintosh (British model), Tiffany Pollard (I Love New York), Calum Best (Famously Single season 1) and Dorothy Wang (#RichKids of Beverly Hills).

The cast members have all had their share of high-profile relationships that went south. Johnson went down swinging (literally) while fighting for JoJo Fletcher‘s affections on The Bachelorette earlier this year before sparking up a volatile and brief romance with Lace Morris on Bachelor in Paradise season 3 this summer. 

Ortiz-Magro split for good with Jersey Shore costar Sammi “Sweetheart” Giancola in 2014, Pollard was the runner-up for Flava Flav‘s heart on Flavor of Love season 1 that aired in 2006, and Smirnoff was engaged to fellow DWTS pro Maksim Chmerkovskiy before they called it off in 2009.

Famously Single‘s first season set the bar pretty high, as Ortiz-Magro’s former Jersey Shore costar Paul “DJ Pauly D” DelVecchio and Aubrey O’Day started dating during filming and are still going strong.
