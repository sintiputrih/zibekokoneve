Who is Tekashi 6ix9ine's ex-girlfriend, Jade?

Tekashi 6ix9ine used to be in a long-term relationship with Rachel Wattley, who goes by Jade.

She is known as a social media influencer, bartender, and aspiring rapper.

In August 2022, she made headlines after it was revealed she was arrested in Miami.

According to TMZ, she was arrested on a battery charge related to a domestic violence incident.

Footage obtained by the media outlet showed the then-couple engaged in a verbal argument and the police report stated that he told cops she hit him.

However, he did not want to cooperate with cops, TMZ notes.

The rapper later told TMZ that he was working on getting his girlfriend of four years out of jail.

"I’m gonna get her out jail -- she attacked me in front of the police I told them, 'You have to evaluate her she’s obviously under the influence I don’t plan to press charges ... I’m the one trying to bail her out," he said at the time.

Outside of her high-profile relationship and criminal charges, Jade is often seen posting about her life on Instagram to over 1.7million followers.

In September 2022, Jade was released and legally barred from contacting 69 via phone calls, texts, letters, or social media, according to TMZ.

She was also ordered to stay at least 500 feet away from him.

However, sources close to the rapper told the outlet: "his relationship with Jade is fine, and he's allowing Jade and her child to stay in his Florida home until the case is resolved."

"69 tells us he will shack up in a hotel as the legal process plays out, and when it's over he will move back in with Jade and her kid."

Were Jade and Tekashi 6ix9ine ever engaged?

Since the duo started their on-again, off-again relationship, Jade and Tekashi fans have been wondering if they plan on walking down the aisle anytime soon.

At this time, the two are not engaged, however, she was spotted in September 2020 trying on diamond rings, according to The Blast.

At the time, the outlet reported that the rings were large in size.

Jade and Tekashi met just two weeks before he was indicted on federal charges of racketeering and a firearms charge stemming from his time in a New York gang.

She previously wrote on Instagram: "He was exceptionally respectful, outgoing, very sweet, kind-hearted and a genuine generous person."

"Yes, we knew each other for a few weeks before his indictment but I decided to stick by his side not because I felt I needed to but that he needed someone he can trust in his corner.”

Who is Tekashi 6ix9ine dating now?

Since mid-June 2023, Tekashi 6ix9ine has reportedly been dating Jorgina Lulú Guillermo Díaz.

She is known professionally as Yailin La Más Viral, and was born on July 4, 2002, in Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic.

Yailin is a rapper, singer, and dembow artist.

She was previously linked to Puerto Rican rapper and singer, Anuel AA.

Viral and Tekashi initially said they were just working on music together, collaborating on the rapper's song, Pa Ti.

They later sparked dating rumors after posting multiple videos on their Instagram accounts in the summer of 2023.

Tekashi was seen gifting Viral stacks of cash, luxury handbags, a Rolex, flowers, and cake for her birthday.

By the end of July, the rapper also gave Viral a black Rolls-Royce.

While the two were able to keep their relationship out of the public eye for a while, they made headlines again in October 2023.

Tekashi was arrested in the Dominican Republic for assault.

The rapper had initially gone to a music studio with a few friends to try and locate Viral, and when he couldn't find her, he allegedly assaulted her music producers.

One of the producers, Diamond La Mafia, alleged that Tekashi was jealous of the amount of time Viral was spending in the studio with him.

Just a few months later, Tekashi made headlines again – but this time, it was his girlfriend, Viral, who was under arrest.

On December 15, 2023, TMZ reported that Yailin had been arrested at the rapper's Florida home for assaulting him and his Bentley.

The two had gotten in a verbal argument that allegedly escalated into a physical attack, though the rapper told police that it was Viral who hit him, and not the other way around.

Neither Tekashi nor Viral have commented on the arrest.
