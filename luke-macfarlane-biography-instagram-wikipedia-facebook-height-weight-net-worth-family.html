Luke Macfarlane, a former singer who is now an actor, is of Canadian and American descent. He was born on January 19, 1980, in London, Canada. Macfarlane came out as gay in an interview with The Globe and Mail on April 15, 2008. On June 12, 2018, Macfarlane was naturalized as a citizen of the United States.

Luke Macfarlane’s Wikipedia

Thomas Luke Macfarlane is a Canadian-American actor and former singer. He is best known for his roles as Scotty Wandell on the ABC drama Brothers & Sisters, RAC Agent D’avin Jaqobis on the Space television science fiction series Killjoys, and the love interest in several Hallmark Channel movies.
