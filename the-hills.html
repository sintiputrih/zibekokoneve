'The Hills' Latest News and Updates

The reality series The Hills premiered in 2006 and was a spinoff of the popular show Laguna Beach, and followed reality star Lauren Conrad as she pursued a career in fashion. 

The Hills' original cast featured Lauren, her BFFs Heidi Montag, Audrina Patridge, and Whitney Port. The supporting cast went on to include Spencer Pratt, Brody Jenner, Justin Bobby, Lo Bosworth, and Stephanie Pratt. 

Lauren left the show during the fifth season and was replaced by former Laguna Beach star Kristin Cavallari. Lauren briefly returned to the series for the finale's alternate ending. The show came to end in 2010. 

In 2019, a reboot of the series The Hills: New Beginnings premiered on the network but did not feature Lauren Conrad or Kristin Cavallari. Instead, former The O.C. star Mischa Barton joined the cast alongside Tommy Lee and Pamela Anderson's son Brandon Thomas Lee. Mischa left the reality series after just one season.
