Kris Jenner Involved in Car Accident, Is OK

Kris Jenner was involved in a car accident off the 101 freeway in Calabasas, California, on Wednesday, August 3. According to Sergeant Covarrubias of the Sheriff’s Department, the momager was driving her white Rolls-Royce when a vehicle cut in front of her and collided with her vehicle.

Jenner — who TMZ reports may have broken her wrist from the impact — was treated for injuries at the scene, Covarrubias tells Us.

Her youngest daughter, Kylie Jenner, tweeted moments after the news broke of the car accident that her mom was going to be fine. The Sheriff’s Department told Us Weekly that Kylie was on hand to pick up her mother and that Kris was the only person in the vehicle. 

“I was not involved in the accident. Everything’s ok ❤️,” Kylie initially tweeted at a concerned fan. “She’s okay!” she added, confirming that Kris, 60, wasn’t seriously injured.

The Kardashians haven’t had the best luck with cars in recent years.
