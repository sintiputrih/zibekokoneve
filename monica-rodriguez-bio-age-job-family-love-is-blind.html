Who Is Monica Rodriguez? Her Age, Job, Family

Monica Rodriguez has dated “all types of men,” but never the type of men who stay. So, searching for the one who’ll never leave, she eventually found herself in Love Is Blind season 4.

This Monica Rodriguez Bio explores her life.

Monica Rodriguez On Love Is Blind Season 4

Netflix’s Love Is Blind season 4 premiered on March 24, 2023, and for the first time in its history, the singles were searching for love on the west coast. However, as always the theme remained the same — 30 new participants including Monica Rodriguez, entered the pod in search of love while only listening to their potential other’s voices.

“Netflix knows how to say happy birthday! Excited to share that I’m a part of the season 4 cast for @loveisblindnetflix! Tune in on March 24th for the premiere of Love is Blind. “The pods are now open,” Monica promoted the show on her IG.

Talking about the experience, she said that she had a fantastic time for which she was “grateful” to the show. Also, the star added that the pods were full of “beautiful human beings” —  and she is one of them.

For those of you new to the show, Love Is Blind follows singles who introduce themselves through their respective pods, unable to see each other, but, able to communicate as much as they want. So, once they’ve decided, the potential husband/wife would then embark on a trip to a romantic destination, and meet each other’s family before deciding if they want to get married in the final ep or not.

Joining Monica for a chance at love were her co-stars Irina Solomonova, Marshall Glaze, Amber Wilder, Kwame Appiah, Micah Lussier, Zack Goytowski, Jackelina Bonds, Brett Brown, Brandie Bowman, Ava Van Jenson, Bill, Bliss Poureetezadi, Chris Clemens, Chelsea Griffin, Conner Fremmerlid, Jack Bonner, Jimmy Forde, Josh Demas, Juan Johnson, and 10 others.

What Is Monica Rodriguez Ideal Boyfriend?

Monica Rodriguez has “all types of men.” So, she knows exactly what she wants in a relationship. A man to grow “with” instead of “out of,” as per Netflix.

Plus, having been cheated on in previous relationships, she was looking for a husband who can make her feel “safe and accepted” and also make her laugh. “The key to my heart is making me laugh and feeling accepted,” she said.

Her biggest pet peeve is bad etiquette and loud talkers.

Monica Rodriguez Age

Monica Rodriguez was 32 years of age when she appeared in Love Is Blind season 4 premiered in March 2023. However, she was a year younger when actually filming the show.

She is the same age as her costars Josh (project engineer), Zack (criminal defense attorney), Chelsea (pediatric speech-language pathologist), and Kacia (family support specialist).
