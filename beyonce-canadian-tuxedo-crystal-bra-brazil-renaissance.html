Beyonc Glams Up a Canadian Tuxedo With a Crystal-Encrusted Bra

Beyoncé took to Instagram to clue her hundreds of thousands of followers into her latest travel fashion. While many fans may have been expecting sweatpants—which only she could make look so luxe—they were in for a surprise, as Queen Bey flew private to Brazil in the most glamorous Canadian tuxedo.

The “Cuff It” singer put a sexy spin on the controversial trend with a light-wash bedazzled jacket that hit her mid-thigh, matching jewel-decorated straight-leg jeans, and the shining star of the show: a crystal-encrusted bra with a strap along the bottom, also adorned with jewels. She was showing love to the country she was visiting, as the fabulous all-denim ensemble comes from Brazilian-founded brand PatBO.

To keep the opulence going, Beyoncé paired the look with strappy, pointed-toe silver slingbacks by the Attico, chunky silver hoop earrings by Jlani, and white-framed cat-eye sunglasses. She showcased her natural beauty with minimal makeup, including a nude lip and mascara, and styled her long platinum blonde hair in crimps, which she wore down and parted in the middle.

In the carousel of images, Beyoncé strikes several poses inside and outside her aircraft. In one, she is pictured with Jay-Z, her husband of 15 years, while in others she holds the Brazilian flag. The pop superstar included a selfie in the last slide, in which she dons a more typical airplane fit—a black zip-up hoodie—while going makeup-free and letting her hair down.

Beyoncé traveled to Brazil to celebrate the country’s premiere of Renaissance: A Film by Beyoncé. She surprised her fans at the “Club Renaissance” party in Salvador, the capital of the state of Bahia.

“It was very important for me to be here, right here in Bahia,” Beyoncé says onstage in a video posted to X. “The Renaissance is about freedom. It’s about beauty, joy, resilience—everything that you are about. I’m so honored to be here, so happy to see your beautiful faces.”

For the occasion, she stunned in head-to-toe metallic silver composed of a sweeping gown that featured a soaring thigh-high slit, ruching, a caped head covering, and sequins all over. She paired the shining dress with matching glittery pointed-toe pumps.
