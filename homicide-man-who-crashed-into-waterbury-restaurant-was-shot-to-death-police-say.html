Homicide: Man Who Crashed Into Waterbury Restaurant Was Shot To Death, Police Say

The incident occurred in New Haven County around 2 a.m., Friday, June 30 at 807 North Main St., at the Ingprica Bar and Restaurant in Waterbury.

According to Lt. Ryan Bessette, of the Waterbury Police, responding officers found that a vehicle had crashed into the restaurant and found an unidentified man inside the vehicle suffering from gunshot wounds.

He was later pronounced dead on the scene, Bessette said.

This is an active and ongoing investigation.

WPD Major Crimes detectives are actively investigating this homicide incident. 

Anyone with information is requested to contact the WPD Detective Bureau at 203-574-6941 or Crime Stoppers anonymous tip line at 203=755-1234.
