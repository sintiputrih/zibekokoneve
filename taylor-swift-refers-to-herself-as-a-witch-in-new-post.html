Taylor Swift Refers To Herself As A 'Witch' In New Post

Quite possibly one of the most outstanding moments in our prolonged period of quarantine had to be when Taylor Swift released her surprise album folklore. The second most outstanding moment? When she released the second surprise album evermore a mere five months later giving us the ultimate treat.

Since the release of both her albums, Swift has been hitting Instagram hard in promoting her work and different versions of songs on the album.

RELATED: Taylor Swift Is Drinking Wine On Zoom

Just this morning, Swift took to her Instagram account to publish a beautiful photo of herself in a quaint room, wearing a simple sweater and her hair in a messy braid captioned, "Witches be like “Sometimes I just want to listen to music while pining away/sulking/staring out a window.” It’s me. I’m witches. Never fear, the “willow lonely witch remix” is here". The post is sharing news of a stripped back version of the single "Willow" off of the evermore album which is full of witch themes.

It is clear that "Willow" is dedicated to those of us who are lonely (and dramatic!) and love to zone out while we immerse ourselves in weepy, sad tunes. And it's great to hear that Taylor is just like us!

This wouldn't be the first time that Swift gets "witchy," TayTay's entire 2020 album evermore is chock-full of witch motifs and fully embraced the vibe of wooden cabins and special fairies. In fact, both evermore and folklore invoke imagery of the woods, wispy details, and feminine mystery.
