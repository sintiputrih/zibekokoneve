Miles Teller and Keleigh Sperry were married in 2019. See pics of them here.

The name Miles Teller might sound familiar. His face might just look even more familiar as well! If so, it is because you have seen this Hollywood hottie’s face on the TV screen, big screen, and every screen in between. Miles Teller is an award-winning actor who has starred in movies, such as The Spectacular Now, Fantastic Four, Divergent, and Whiplash.

He married his beautiful wife (and long-time girlfriend) Keleigh Rand Sperry on September 1, 2019. For those of you not all too familiar with Keleigh Sperry, she is an Orange County, California native who was born on October 16, 1992. She is best known for her career as an actress and TV personality, in which she has appeared on Dance (2017) and E! Live from the Red Carpet (1995).  The couple also appeared in Taylor Swift’s 2021 music video for “I Bet You Think About Me.”
