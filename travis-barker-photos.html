Photos of the Blink-182 Drummer Hollywood Life

Punk rock royalty! Celebrate Travis Barker’s birthday by taking a look at photos of him through the years, here.

Blink 182 drummer Travis Barker has lived quite the life, going from a scrappy young rocker to the world-famous husband of Kourtney Kardashian. He started making music at an early age and joined the ska outfit The Aquabats at 21 in 1996, only to leave 2 years later to join Blink. The “All The Small Things” musicians would find international fame with 1999’s Enema Of The State. He would go on to collaborate with artists across genres, dabbling in hip-hop, alternative rock, pop, and country. While touring the globe, Travis had a near-death plane crash experience in 2008 which left 4 others dead. The star shares children Landon, 18, and Alabama, 15, with ex-wife Shanna Moakler. The pair were married in 2004 and filed for divorce in 2016, only to briefly reconcile before officially pulling the plug on the relationship in 2008. He began dating Kourtney Kardashian in January 2021 and the pair were engaged that October. The couple got married in Italy on May 22, 2022.
