Is Karen Kilgariff Married? Who Is Her Husband? Details Of Her Personal Life!

American comedian, writer, author, actress, singer, television producer, and host Karen Kilgariff, who started professional career in 1990, openly talks about her professional journey activities but keeps lips sealed when talked about her personal life.

Everyone is familiar with the fact that Karen was once a married woman. So, what about now? What is her former husband doing now?

Who Is Karen Married To Now?

After separating from former husband Thompson, Karen has been maintaining a low-key profile. She has not talked anything about her current relationship status.

As the writer is not linked with anyone or has ever seen spending cozy time with anyone, many sources reported that she is single. According to gossip mongers, she currently lives in her California mansion with her two pet dogs, Frank and George.

Struggles With Addiction

While growing up, Karen developed addiction problems. She was an alcoholic and was struggling with amphetamines. But, as of now, she is sober and has been sober for many years. She once shared that she suffered seizures, so it motivated her to stay sober.

As of now, she lives with her pet dogs and is enjoying quality time with them.

How Was Her Previous Married Life?

Karen was once a married woman. As per the official court documents, she was married to Peter S. Thompson. It is not clear when they tied the knot, but it was reported that they filed for divorce on 18th January 2013. The duo also welcomed children and even had fought for their kids’ custody.

According to sources, Karen and her former husband first met while working at The Ellen DeGeneres Show. Her ex-husband is a cameraman and photographer who has worked in many commercials, television shows, videos, and series.

For now, it is not clear whether both Karen and Thompson moved on or still single.

Professional Career And Net Worth!

Karen started her professional career in 1990 and has been actively working since then. Back in the 1990s, she began working as a stand-up comedian in Sacramento.

As a writer, she has written for shows like Ellen DeGeneres: Here and Now, The Rosie Show, Talk Show: The Game Show, Baskets, The Pete Holmes Show, and many others.

Karen started podcast hosting in 2014 with Chris Fairbanks, Do You Need A Ride? As an author, she has written a joint memoir, Stay Sexy and Don’t Get Murdered,  with Georgia Hardstark.

As of 2021, Karen Kilgariff’s net worth is reported to be around $20 million.
