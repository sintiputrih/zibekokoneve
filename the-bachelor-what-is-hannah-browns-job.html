What Is Hannah Browns Job?

Reality TV fans often joke about the careers explained on each contestant's title cards on The Bachelor. Sometimes people are called "entrepreneurs," which is innocent enough but sometimes meaningless, but according to Entertainment Weekly, the job titles have been "construction consultant" and "twin."

Hannah Brown is a favorite to appear on the show and get her shot at The Bachelorette. She is someone people can relate to, whether she's sharing about her depression or hanging out with her dad.

Brown is only 26 years old and while fans are eager to see her find love and live happily ever after, what kind of career does she have? Let's take a look.

Brown's Job

Even though Hannah Brown is super famous now, she has realized that some things are off-limits, like a fancy and private dating app that wouldn't say yes to her.

Fans are curious about what she does for a living (besides her appearances on reality TV, of course).

Brown was an interior decorator. According to Distractify.com, she was employed by a company called Gracefully Done. She wrote on her LinkedIn that she has "combined my knowledge in communication within my passion with design and pursued a career in interior design at Gracefully Done in Northport, Alabama."

Brown continued that she likes "forming relationships with my clients and being able to meet specific needs for creating their dream space.” In May 2017, she finished school at the University of Alabama, and after doing her work-study at the firm, she started getting clients and it sounds like she loved it.

Brown has decorated in a "modern" and also "shabby chic" style. According to Refinery 29, her company's website explains that the firm likes to design in styles called "Rustic Elegance" and "French Country Charm." Some of the rooms that she designed can be found on her Instagram feed.

Pageant Life

Fans of The Bachelor and The Bachelorette know that Hannah Brown has also been a beauty queen and that this was a big part of her life.

According to ET Online, Brown won the title of Miss Steel City in 2014, and then she went to college. She began her pageant career when she was 15 years old and although she didn't win anything at the 2013 Miss Alabama contest, she won 2012's Miss Tuscaloosa pageant. Other wins include being a second runner-up in the 2011 contest for Miss Alabama Teen USA and getting first runner-up for the 2010 Miss Alabama Outstanding Teen contest. She was crowned Miss Alabama in 2018.

Her Path Now

What is Hannah Brown going to do now? Some fans have said that Brown would be amazing if she was on season 16 of The Bachelorette. According to Cheat Sheet, host Chris Harrison says that he understands why people wish for that as Brown is so great. He said, in an interview with Us Weekly, "Look, anything’s possible. I’m not against it. I think the things that make us love her so much — and I’m included in that and adore her — is she is so open and honest and just, she’s a train wreck. You get to see the good, the bad and the ugly in Hannah and that’s a rare thing in any human being.”

In August 2020, she shared with her Instagram followers that she was feeling emotional on the one year anniversary of her storyline on The Bachelorette, according to People.

Brown wrote, “Today feels really symbolic for me. As I get ready to move into a new home, I realize that I am starting a new chapter and closing another. (Which is a big deal because I’m usually rushing to the next chapter before I finish the one before).”

Brown continued that she didn't have any regrets and didn't wish that things had gone differently. She wrote, "I’m thankful for every single one of my experiences and the subsequent moments of growth, and of course, I’m thankful for all of you! Tough times don’t feel so rough when you have some pretty great friends on the internet rooting for ya along the way! Cheers, to new beginnings y’all!” It sounds like Brown learned some great lessons on the reality show and will bring those with her.

It'll be interesting to see where Hannah Brown goes next and what kind of job she has in her future. On her Instagram bio, she says "I'm still Alabama Hannah" so one thing is for sure, no matter what she does now or how famous she gets, she hasn't forgotten where she comes from. And that's just one reason why she is so popular.
