Percy Hynes White career earnings, salary and net worth

Percy Hynes White was born in St. John’s, Canada on October 8, 2001. Award-winning film and television actor with credits in The Grand Seduction, A Christmas Horror Story, and Cast No Shadow. He began starring as Andy Strucker in FOX’s The Gifted in 2018.

He also appeared in the third installment of the Night at the Museum franchise. 2016 was a big year for him, starring in films titled Rupture, Edge of Winter, Milton’s Secret, and First Round Down, the latter of which starred Dylan Bruce.
