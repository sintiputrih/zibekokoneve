Jake Weary Biography; Net Worth, Height, Interview, Animal Kingdom And Movies

Jake Weary is an American actor, musician, songwriter and music producer. He was born on February 14, 1990, in Trenton, New Jersey to Kim Zimmer and Allen Cudney.

Jake began his career when he made his first appearance on CBS soap opera, Guiding Light in 2002, where his mother, actress Kim Zimmer, played the role of Reva Shayne. Jake currently features in criminal drama, Animal Kingdom.

Aside from his acting career, Jake Weary has been writing, producing and recording his own music since he was 12 years old. He released a single titled EP in July 2011 and a sophomore effort titled Tones in June 2012. Weary currently produces music under his names.

Jake Weary Net Worth

Jake Weary has an estimated net worth of $1.6 million. He earned this through his career as an actor and a musician.
