Biography

Chris Carpentier was born in Chile on August 23, 1973. Professional chef who is recognized for being a judge on the television series MasterChef Chile. He is also known for owning the restaurant El Barrio and Maldito Chef. He has also worked at the Hotel Faena in Buenos Aires as the head chef. He, Yann Yvin, and Sergi Arola are all judges for MasterChef Chile. He studied cooking in Washington D.C. at the age of 18. He opened the restaurant Water in Santiago which earned him recognition in Conde Nast Magazine. He began appearing in television programs on the Channel 13 and RCN networks and was selected to judge MasterChef Chile in 2014.
