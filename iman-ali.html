Iman Ali is a Pakistani actress and international supermodel. She is best known for playing the role of ‘Maryam’ (Mary) in the Urdu film “Khuda Key Liye.” She is one of the highest paid actresses of Pakistani Cinema.

Wiki/Biography

Iman Ali was born on 19 December 1977 (age 41 years; as in 2018) in Lahore, Pakistan. She did her schooling from Divisional Public School (DPS), Model Town, Lahore. After completing her studies, she went to pursue her career as a model. Iman started her modelling career by doing a number of TV commercials.

Career

Iman made her acting debut in 2008 with the TV show “Dil Dekay Jaien Gey.” Subsequently, she appeared in TV serials like “Arman,” “Kismat,” “Woh Tees Din,” “Pehla Pyar,” and “Kuch Log Rooth Kar Kay Bhi.” In 2007, she made her Urdu film debut by playing the lead role in the film “Khuda Kay Liye.” The movie was a big hit and her performance was critically acclaimed. Next, she featured in the film “Bol” in which she played the supporting role of Meena (the tawaif).

In 2015, Ali appeared in the film “Mah-E-Mir” alongside Fahad Mustafa and Sanam Saeed.
