Natasha Lyonne bio, family, career, net worth, boyfriend

Who is Natasha Lyonne?

Natasha Lyonne, born Natasha Bianca Lyonne Braunstein, is an actress, director, producer, and writer from the United States. Lyonne’s success came primarily as a result of her participation in the wildly popular Netflix comedy series Orange is the New Black.

Natasha Lyonne bio

Natasha Lyonne was born on April 4, 1979, making her 43 years old as of 2022. She was raised in New York, New York, where she was born. She is presently a resident of California’s Los Angeles. She is an American citizen who practices Ashkenazi Jewish Judaism.

Natasha Lyonne career

Lyonne received her first acting break in the comedy-drama Heartburn in 1986, playing Rachel’s niece without receiving any credit for the part. In the late 1990s, she began to feature in a number of films, including Christine Sixteen in 1999’s Detroit Rock City and Woody Allen’s Everyone Says I Love You in 1996.

She also played Polly in 1993’s Dennis the Menace and Djuna “DJ” Berlin in 1996’s Dennis the Menace. Lyonne also starred as Jessica in the 1999 movie American Pie, which went on to become a worldwide smash hit and earn over $235.5 million.

Following the enormous popularity of her debut performance, Natasha reprised her Jessica character in American Pie 2 in 2001 and American Reunion in 2012. At the box office, both movies brought in more than $200 million.

She appeared in numerous movies in 2001, including Tamara Jenson’s Fast Sofa, Megan Voorhees’ Scary Movie 2 and Darci’s Kate & Leopold.

Natasha went on to appear in a number of films, such as Comic Book Villains (2002), Zig Zag (2002), Night at the Golden Eagle, Die (2002), Mommie, Die! (2003), Party Monster (2003), Madhouse (2004), Blade: Trinity (2004), Robots (2005), My Suicidal Sweetheart (2005), The Immaculate Conception of Little Dizzle (2009), and Goyband (2009).

Lyonne joined the cast of the horror movie All About Evil in 2010, as Deborah Tennis, a librarian turned serial murderer. She then starred in movies like He’s Way More Famous Than You (2013), 4:44 Last Day on Earth (2011), Night Club (2011), and Kaplan in Loitering with Intent.

She also co-starred in Girl Most Likely (2013) with Annette Bening and Kristen Wiig (2014). Natasha made her cinematic debut in Addicted to Fresno, Sleeping with Other People, Bloomin’ Mud Shuffle, Hello, My Name Is Doris, and Emma in #Horror in 2015. She also made appearances in the following 5 other films.

Natasha Lyonne not only acted in movies, but she also starred in a lot of television programs. She appeared on television for the first time in Pee-Playhouse wee’s in 1986, appearing in all six episodes as Opal.

She started appearing on the show as a guest star in 2000 and didn’t act on television again until. She first appeared in the television movie If These Walls Could Talk 2 as Jeanne after guest starring as Gilla on Will & Grace in 2000.

Lyonne continued to appear as a guest star in a number of TV shows, such as Weeds (2012), Grounded for Life (2012), The Knights of Prosperity (2007), Loving Leah (2009), New Girl (2011), and Law & Order: Special Victims Unit (2011).

Natasha Lyonne’s role as drug addict Nicky Nichols on the Netflix series Orange Is the New Black, which she appeared in for more than 7 seasons and 81 episodes from 2013 to 2019, marked her biggest professional breakthrough.

She also directed The Hidey Hole, an episode from this series’ first seven seasons. When Lyonne started playing Nadia Vulvokov on the highly acclaimed comedy-drama TV series Russian Doll in 2019, she furthered her notoriety.

Natasha Lyonne relationship

Natasha Lyonne and actor Edward Furlong began dating in 1998; however, they broke up the following year. The relationship between the actress and American actor Adam Goldberg, which started later in 2001, did not endure long; it ended in 2004. She had a brief romance with actor Michael Rapaport in the year 2005.

Natasha started dating actor, comedian, singer, and playwright Fred Armisen in August of 2014. Lyonne is around 13 years older than Fred, who is well-known for Portlandia and Saturday Night Live. The pair decided to end their relationship after more than 8 years together. Natasha said that the two had split up in April 2022.
