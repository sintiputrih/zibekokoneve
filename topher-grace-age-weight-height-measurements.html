Topher Grace Age, Weight, Height, Measurements

Biography:

Topher Grace was born on July 12, 1978 in New York City, New York, USA. He made his television debut as part of the main cast of the hit series That ’70s Show in 1998. He played the part until the finale in 2006. In 2000, he also made a memorable debut in the acclaimed movie Traffic. While working on That ’70s Show, he played a supporting role in Mona Lisa Smile and starred in Win a Date with Tad Hamilton!.

He went on to star in the movies P.S. and In Good Company. In 2007, he achieved further prominence after landing the role of Eddie Brock/Venom in Spider-Man 3. In the 2010s, he starred in Valentine’s Day, Predators, The Double, The Calling, and Interstellar. He also ventured into writing and producing with the 2011 movie Take Me Home Tonight, a film in which he also starred in. He recently starred in the movie American Ultra.
