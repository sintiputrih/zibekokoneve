Turner &amp; Hooch Photos Of Josh Pecks Reboot Of The Classic Movie Hollywood Life

‘Turner & Hooch’ is a new Disney+ series starring Josh Peck. Josh stars as Scott Turner, son of Detective Scott Turner portrayed by Tom Hanks in the 1989 film of the same name. Like his father before him, Scott’s life is turned upside down when the US Marshal inherits a big, unruly dog. It turns out, Hooch is just the partner Scott needs. The series also stars Carra Patterson as Jessica Baxter, Scott’s brave, street-smart partner; Brandon Jay McLaren as Xavier Wilson, a cool, enigmatic marine-turned-US Marshal; Anthony Ruivivar as Chief James Mendez, Scott’s boss with a secret soft spot for Scott’s new dog, Hooch; Lyndsy Fonseca as Laura Turner, Scott’s sweet, animal-loving sister; Jeremy Maguire as Matthew Garland, Laura’s dog-loving son; and Vanessa Lengies as Erica Mounir, the quirky head of the US Marshals dog training program.
