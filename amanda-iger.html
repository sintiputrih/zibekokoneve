He also increased Disney’s presence in international markets and grew the company’s intellectual property portfolio.

He also had a key role in the revitalization of Walt Disney Animation Studios, the brand-release strategy for the studio’s film production, and the company’s increasing investment in its direct-to-consumer operations, such as Disney+ and Hulu.
