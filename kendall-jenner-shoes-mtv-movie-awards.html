Kendall Jenner Struggled With Her MTV Movie Awards Shoes

In case you haven't already seen, Kendall Jenner rocked the red carpet at the MTV Movie Awards last night in a sultry all-black look, accented with some major laces. Turns out, her killer thigh-high lace-up DSquared2 sandals were not a breeze to wear (it apparently took a whole team to get her into them). 

Her date to the show, fellow It model Gigi Hadid, hilariously documented the struggle—as best friends do—on Snapchat, showing Jenner's unraveled laces getting stuck in an elevator. On her own Snap, Jenner showed herself attempting to take her shoes off in the car, to no avail. She commented it would probably take an hour to take her shoes off—we wouldn't be surprised if it did.
