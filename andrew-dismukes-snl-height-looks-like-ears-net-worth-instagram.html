Andrew Dismukes (SNL)

Andrew Vincent Dismukes is an American comedian.

He has been a writer on Saturday Night Live since 2017 and a cast member of the 46th season since 2020.

Andrew Dismukes was born on June 21, 1995, in Port Neches, Texas to Lori Ann Dismukes and Gregory Dismukes.

Dismukes graduated from Port Neches-Groves High School in 2013.

He subsequently graduated from the University of Texas at Austin in 2017 with a Bachelor of Science degree in Radio-Television-Film.

Dismukes began performing a stand-up comedy in 2013, while in college.

He was the runner-up “Funniest Person in Austin” in 2016, a contest run by the Cap City Comedy Club.
