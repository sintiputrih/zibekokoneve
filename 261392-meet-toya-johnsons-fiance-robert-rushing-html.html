Meet Toya Johnson's Fianc Robert Rushing with Whom She Shares Daughter Reign

Toya Johnson has found love in Robert Rushing. He is her daughter's father, but there are many things about him people don't know.

Toya Johnson has always been tight-lipped about her love life. She got involved with Robert Rushing in 2017 but was very particular about keeping the details private.

Toya Johnson at the Leaders and Legends Ball on January 15, 2020. | Photo: Getty Images

She has also been publicly marking milestones with him. He clocked 40 earlier this year and she threw him a lavish star-studded dinner, and well, all that love has her fans curious about who he is and what he does.

Like Johnson, Rushing is very particular about living a private life. He was born in Atlanta and is reportedly a former athlete who played for the Troy State Trojans while attending Troy University in Alabama. During his time, he played point guard and played it well.

HIS CAREER

His love for the sport may be what inspired him to become a sports manager for athletes. He has made a name for himself in the business and has reportedly represented stars like Dwight Howard and Tristan Thompson.

The proposal caught her unawares.

Rushing is also the founder of the clothing line "2 Commas"— a brand inspired by the American dream. He has ventured into some other businesses, but right now, his focus seems to be on one particular project.

A COUPLE THAT WORKS TOGETHER…

Rushing and his wife launched a health and fitness company called "Weight No More" in 2019, and they have been recording success. The couple has hosted fitness events in different states and sells their athletic clothing and fitness products on their website.

THEIR RELATIONSHIP

As earlier stated, Rushing and Johnson started dating in 2017, and they got engaged in 2019. The proposal caught her unawares, and the method the entrepreneur employed melted his partner's heart.

Aside from the daughter the two share, Johnson has a grown daughter named Reginae. She shares her with her former husband, Lil Wayne.

Aside from him, she has been romantically linked to producer Memphitz. Fans of the couple can't wait to hear the wedding bells ring for them, and perhaps later, more feet will run in the halls of their home.
